Biography

I am currently a professor at the Laboratoire J.-L. Lagrange, a joint laboratory between Université Côte d’Azur, CNRS and Observatoire de la Côte d’Azur.

My current research activity is centered around statistical data processing, inverse problems and machine learning. I have a particular interest in applications to Astrophysics, such as radio interferometry.
